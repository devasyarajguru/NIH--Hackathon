Child Marriage Laws:

  • Explains the legal age of marriage for boys and girls in India.
  • Discusses the consequences of child marriage and the efforts to eradicate it.

In India, legal services for child marriage laws are primarily aimed at preventing underage marriages. Parents seeking information or legal assistance can contact local government authorities or child protection organizations, such as the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) and the Ministry of Women and Child Development. They can also seek guidance from legal aid clinics and NGOs specializing in child rights. It's essential for parents to be aware of the legal provisions in the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006, which prohibits marriages involving minors. To learn more, visit the official NCPCR website or consult with a legal expert.
